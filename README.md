A base defence/crafting sruvival game developed for a university course.
- Fight animated junk creatures
- Salvage scrap components
- Explore the junkyard for resources
- Prevent smaller “trashlings” from merging into more complex creatures
- Craft makeshift weapons and barricades
- Defend and manage your oxygen base

## 🧩Background 
You clock in for your weekly shift on Scraptis Prime, managing an isolated junkyard on a remote 
dwarf planet. Upon your arrival, you trigger a hidden relic from a old ancient war between humans and aliens. The artefact creates a storm which disables your ship and is now animating piles of junk into hostile junk creatures. The player must wait and protect the ship while it recovers so that they can escape. To survive, the player must salvage parts from defeated enemies and explore the junkyard to craft improvised weapons and defenses while protecting their ship base from increasingly dangerous threats.
